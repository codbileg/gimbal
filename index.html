BUT

Not everyone was lost that day.

1. I can't find my key

That morning, a woman couldn't find her keys. The delay made her late for work. She never reached her office in the World Trade Center. A few lost minutes saved her life.

2. I missed my metro

He missed his train that morning. Just a few minutes behind schedule, he never made it to the towers before the attacks. A small delay saved his life.

3. I wanna drink coffee

She left the building for a cup of coffee. Minutes later, the attacks began. A routine morning habit became the reason she survived.

Small moments changed entire lives.

Perhaps some things happen for a reason, even when we do not understand why.
